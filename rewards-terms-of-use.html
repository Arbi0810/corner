Becoming a Catering Rewards Member

CBC’s Catering Rewards Program allows you to earn and receive rewards (rewards, including Corner Cash and Amazon.com Gift Cards, are individually or collectively referred to as “Rewards(s)”). To become a Program member, you must: (1) be at least 18 years old, (2) register for a Catering Rewards account at https://www.cornerbakerycafe.com/cateringrewards, and (3) be subscribed to the Catering Rewards Program email list. Your Catering Rewards account is personal to you and may not be sold, transferred, or assigned to, or shared with, family, friends, or others, or used by you for any commercial purpose. A unique and valid email address is required to register for a Catering Rewards account. You may be required to be presented the email registered in connection with you Catering Rewards account in connection with a transaction or activity. The Program is not targeted towards, nor intended for use by, anyone under the age of 18. There are no membership fees associated with the Catering Rewards Program.

Earning and Using Rewards

For every $500 you spend in qualifying Corner Bakery Cafe® catering purchases (net sales) using a registered Catering Rewards account, you will receive 1 of 2 pre-selected Rewards: $25 Corner Cash certificate or $10 Amazon.com Gift Card. A qualifying purchase constitutes a catering order that you place (i) in-cafe or via CBC’s catering website, with respect to orders fulfilled by participating Corner Bakery Cafe® locations. The amount of the qualifying purchase equals the amount paid for food and beverages after application of any applicable discounts and/or Rewards redeemed, exclusive of taxes, tips, delivery, cancellation or other fees, and exclusive of gift card purchases. If you have a Corner Bakery Cafe Catering House Account, it must be in good standing to be eligible for earned Rewards.

When earned, you will receive a Reward email notification to the “Rewards Email” provided at registration. Corner Cash certificates are valid on food and beverage purchases made in-cafe at participating cafes. They are not valid towards online catering purchase. Amazon.com Gift Cards may be redeemed at www.amazon.com/redeem. Restrictions apply, see www.amazon.com/gc-legal.com. Amazon Gift Cards and Corner Cash certificates never expire. Rewards are subject to change or substitution without notice. Rewards accumulated under the Catering Rewards Program are promotional and have no cash value. Rewards may not be exchanged for cash, any cash equivalent, or any other products, or combined with other discounts or coupons. Transaction history cannot be transferred from one account to another.
